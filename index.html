About Us

Sisterhood specializes in serving women and aims at empowerment through physical activity, generating self-knowledge, and self-confidence, and reducing stress. We want to be a place where any woman can come in, get fit, and feel good, comfortable, and healthy. We support diversity and accessibility. Our proposal is to offer a humanized service and the best result, for this we provide exclusive training and functional exercises. Sisterhood has a structure designed to make your workouts even more effective, safe, and enjoyable. Our equipment is new, the training sessions were designed exclusively for your needs, humanized service, and close monitoring for your protection during each student's training, here you will no longer be just one, we want to understand your limits, and your goals and work together to achieve them.
