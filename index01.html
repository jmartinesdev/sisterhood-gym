CROSSFIT

What is Cross Training? It combines two or more activities, with more intense exercises that generate high caloric expenditure, but can also be highly recommended for injury prevention. By performing functional, high-intensity activities, practitioners improve physical conditioning, increase muscle definition, and yes, they can lose weight. A cross circuit training or cross-circuit training will allow the practitioner to perform different activities, in addition to using and strengthening other muscle groups. In addition, the cross-training practitioner generates a higher energy consumption than a person who does only one activity, which makes it more efficient but more economical.
